Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

wardrobeAI ("we", "our", or "app") operates the wardrobeAI mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app. By using wardrobeAI, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

wardrobeAI does not collect personal data such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Tracking or analytics data

The app only processes clothing images that users voluntarily upload.

2. Photo Storage

All clothing photos uploaded to wardrobeAI are stored locally on the user's device. We do not store photos on our own servers. We do not maintain user accounts or databases. If the user deletes the app, all data is deleted from the device.

3. AI Processing

To generate outfit suggestions, clothing photos are sent securely to the Google Gemini API for analysis. This process is temporary and only used to provide outfit recommendations.

  • Images are not stored on wardrobeAI servers
  • Images are not used for AI training
  • Images are not shared with advertisers
  • Images are processed only for analysis requests

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. Data may only be disclosed if required by law.

5. Security

We prioritize user privacy and minimal data collection. All possible data is stored locally on the user's device. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

6. Children's Privacy

wardrobeAI is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
